What is an AI Consultant?

An AI consultant helps businesses identify, implement, and optimize artificial intelligence solutions. From AIOS (AI Operating System) installations to chatbot development, workflow automation, and AI strategy, consultants provide the expertise businesses need to adopt AI without the trial and error.

What is AIOS?

AIOS (AI Operating System) is a framework for integrating AI into every layer of a business — from customer service and sales to operations and finance. AIOS consultants build custom AI systems that run continuously, handling tasks that previously required full-time staff.

How to Choose an AI Consultant

  • Check their track record — verified reviews and case studies matter
  • Look for niche experience — an AI consultant who knows your industry will move faster
  • Ask about tools — the best consultants know Claude, OpenAI, local models, and when to use each
  • Get a discovery audit — a good consultant maps your opportunities before pitching solutions
